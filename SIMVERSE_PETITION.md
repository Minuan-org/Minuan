# SimVerse Petition for Children (Ages 5–10)

SimVerse is a kid-powered company created by Commander AbdulSamad (age 8).
It is made for children who love building, imagining, and creating new ideas.

SimVerse is not just one idea. It is a whole universe of ideas, including:

1. A Real-Life 3D Simulator that looks almost like real life.
2. Tools that make life easier for everyone, from babies to grandparents.
3. A new kind of internet that is simple enough for any child to use.
4. HTML6, CSS Next, and JS²
5. A safe place for kids to learn, create, and share ideas.

Who can join SimVerse?
- Children ages 5–10
- Kids who love creating and imagining
- Kids who want to help build the future

Rules:
- If you want to use real tools like Supabase, you must use a parent-made email.
- No strangers allowed.
- Be kind, be creative, and have fun.
- Kids without email can still join the company.
